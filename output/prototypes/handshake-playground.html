How to read this playground

The sticky control bar drives a single shared step value across every direction (0 = pre-flight, 3 = all done). Press ▶ Play transition to walk through the whole journey and watch each direction breathe.

Each row is one design system — the same primitive (segments, rings, pips, etc.) at three sizes. The question to answer when choosing: which one feels like AO, reads instantly at the slim stepper size, and earns a "yes!" at the interstitial moment?
